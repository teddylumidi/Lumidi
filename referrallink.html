Referral Programs & Integrity

Genuine referral links are used by websites to incentivize users to refer others and earn rewards. These programs have criteria to validate referrals and prevent fraud. Websites use tracking mechanisms to ensure the legitimacy of referrals and award benefits accordingly. Referral programs build trust and transparency, ensuring users earn rewards for genuine referrals.
